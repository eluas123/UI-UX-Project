Organization structure

The strongest body in FIFA is the FIFA Congress , which consists of representatives from all football associations and federations that are members of FIFA. Amendments to the FIFA regulations require the approval of the Congress. The members of the Congress elect the President of FIFA and the members of the FIFA Council . The day-to-day management of the federation is the responsibility of the Executive Committee, which is chaired by the President of FIFA. In addition, there are various committees responsible for more focused issues, such as the Finance Committee, the Judges Committee, the Disciplinary Committee, etc.
